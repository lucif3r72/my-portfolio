Talent Acquisition Portal

A dual-platform recruitment system for Anglo-Eastern Shipping Management — a web app for HR teams to manage pipelines and approvals, and a mobile app for candidates to complete applications and participate in AI-powered interviews.

  • CLIENT Anglo-Eastern
  • ROLE UI/UX Designer
  • PLATFORM Web + Mobile
  • STATUS Internal Platform

Overview

Anglo-Eastern's recruitment ran on 10+ paper forms and disconnected systems. HR teams had no efficient way to search vast candidate databases, and approval hierarchies lived in email threads. The goal was to digitize the entire workflow — intelligent search, RBAC-based approvals, and seamless form completion — without breaking from the established design system.

Approach

The web platform centres on an advanced search architecture with maritime-specific filters for precise candidate discovery. Interfaces adapt dynamically based on RBAC permissions, with smart approval routing replacing email chains. Paper forms became progressive, contextual digital experiences. The mobile app integrates an AI conversational interview agent via phone — both platforms sharing the same visual language throughout.

Results

The platform eliminated paper-based delays across the full hiring cycle. Advanced maritime search filters now let HR teams navigate large candidate pools in seconds. RBAC ensures secure, role-appropriate access while automating approval routing. Currently in Phase 3 — expanding social integration and AI automation — targeting measurable gains in recruitment throughput and candidate experience.
