AESM TAP
Talent Acquisition Portal

AESM TAP is a comprehensive talent acquisition platform for Anglo-Eastern Shipping Management. The dual-platform solution includes a web application for HR teams to manage requisitions, candidate pipelines, and hiring workflows, plus a mobile app for candidates to complete applications and participate in AI-powered interviews. The system streamlines recruitment for new hires and existing crew members transitioning between vessels.

  • CLIENT Anglo-Eastern
  • ROLE UI/UX Designer
  • PLATFORM Web App (Admin/HR)
    + Mobile App (Candidates)
  • LIVE PROJECT Internal Platform

Challenges

The existing recruitment process relied on extensive paper-based forms and fragmented systems. HR teams managed 10+ different form types manually with no efficient way to search through massive candidate databases. Complex RBAC requirements created approval workflow bottlenecks. Technical challenges included selecting appropriate technology stacks while adhering to enterprise guidelines and implementing sophisticated search filters for maritime qualifications.

Goals

Our objective was to digitize the entire talent acquisition ecosystem while building upon Anglo-Eastern's established design system. We focused on creating intelligent search across extensive candidate databases, implementing RBAC-based approval workflows, and digitizing paper forms into intuitive digital experiences. The mobile app needed comprehensive form completion with AI-powered conversational interviews. The goal was achieving significant process improvements across all hiring workflows.

Design Highlights

Built on Anglo-Eastern's base design system, the web platform features advanced search architecture with maritime-specific filters for precise candidate discovery. The RBAC system dynamically adjusts interfaces based on permissions, with smart approval routing. Form digitization transformed paper workflows into progressive, contextual experiences. The mobile app integrates AI conversational interviews via phone, while both platforms maintain consistent visual language.

Results

The platform successfully digitized the complete hiring workflow, eliminating paper-based delays. Advanced search capabilities now enable HR teams to efficiently navigate vast candidate pools using maritime-specific filters. The RBAC implementation ensures secure, role-appropriate access while streamlining approval processes. Currently in Phase 3 with social integration and expanded AI automation, we're targeting substantial improvements in recruitment efficiency and user satisfaction.
