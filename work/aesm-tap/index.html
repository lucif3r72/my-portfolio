AESM TAP

Talent Acquisition Portal

CLIENT Anglo-Eastern
ROLE UI/UX Designer
PLATFORM Web + Mobile
STATUS Internal Platform
YEAR 2025
01 — OVERVIEW

The Problem

Anglo-Eastern's recruitment ran on 10+ paper forms and disconnected systems. HR teams had no efficient way to search vast candidate databases, and approval hierarchies lived in email threads.

The goal was to digitise the entire workflow — intelligent search, RBAC-based approvals, and seamless form completion — without breaking from the established design language.

02 — APPROACH

How We Solved It

The web platform centres on an advanced search architecture with maritime-specific filters for precise candidate discovery. Interfaces adapt dynamically based on RBAC permissions, with smart approval routing replacing email chains.

Paper forms became progressive, contextual digital experiences. The mobile app integrates an AI conversational interview agent via phone — both platforms sharing the same visual language throughout.

03 — RESULTS

Outcome

The platform eliminated paper-based delays across the full hiring cycle. Advanced maritime search filters now let HR teams navigate large candidate pools in seconds. RBAC ensures secure, role-appropriate access while automating approval routing.
