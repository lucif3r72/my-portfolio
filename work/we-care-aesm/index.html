WeCare App (Redesign)

WeCare is a mobile app used by seafarers and onshore staff at Anglo-Eastern. It offers wellness tools, training schedules, forums, and ways to appreciate colleagues. The original app was cluttered, hard to navigate, and didn’t match the company’s design standards. I redesigned the entire interface to make it cleaner, easier to use, and more aligned with their brand.

  • CLIENT Anglo-Eastern
  • ROLE UI/UX Designer
  • PLATFORM Mobile (Android & iOS)
  • LIVE PROJECT Visit Site

Challenges

The old app had a dark, crowded layout. Important features were hard to find, and the navigation felt confusing. Tap areas were small, and the design didn’t work well on mobile devices—especially in low-connectivity environments. There was no consistent design system, which made updates difficult.

Goals

My goal was to simplify the experience. I introduced a white background for better clarity, grouped features into logical sections, and created a modular design system. I focused on accessibility, mobile responsiveness, and making key features easy to discover.

Design Highlights

The new design uses clean layouts, semantic colors, and intuitive navigation. Features like AE Live, Medical Contact, and Appreciation are now easy to access. I designed reusable components like cards and buttons, optimized for mobile use. The app feels lighter, faster, and more user-friendly.

Before vs After

Feature Legacy App Redesigned App
Background Dark, cluttered White, breathable
Navigation Scroll-heavy Clustered, bottom nav
Accessibility Low contrast WCAG-compliant
Forum Static Categorized, searchable
Appreciation Hidden Prominent CTA with feedback
AE Live Mixed styles Unified cards with icons

Results

User feedback improved by 42%. Appreciation usage went up 3.5x. Navigation became faster and more intuitive. The design system is now being used for other Anglo-Eastern mobile apps.

Reflection

This redesign was about making life easier for maritime professionals. By focusing on clarity and usability, WeCare became a tool that truly supports its users—whether they’re onboard or onshore.
