Uberheal — Unified Healthcare Operations Platform

Uberheal is a live healthcare operations platform used by clinics and healthcare providers across the United States. It functions as a centralized contact center and operational hub, enabling front desks, care coordinators, referral teams, and campaign managers to handle communication, appointments, providers, and referrals from a single system. The platform is designed exclusively for healthcare staff and is not patient-facing.

  • CLIENT Healthcare SaaS (US)
  • ROLE UI/UX Designer
  • PLATFORM Web Application
  • LIVE PROJECT In Production (US)

Challenges

Healthcare teams rely on multiple disconnected tools for calling, messaging, appointment scheduling, referrals, and campaigns. This fragmentation increases manual work, delays patient outreach, and reduces operational visibility. Designing a single platform that could replace multiple systems—without overwhelming staff—was the core challenge.

Goals

The goal was to unify healthcare operations into one role-based platform. Front desks needed fast scheduling and calling tools, referral teams required structured referral workflows, and campaign managers needed targeted outreach capabilities. The system had to scale from small clinics to multi-location practices while remaining easy to use.

Design Highlights

Uberheal is built around role-based dashboards that reduce cognitive load by surfacing only relevant tools. A unified contact center supports WhatsApp, SMS, AI calling, and manual calls. Campaign managers can create targeted campaigns for appointments and follow-ups, while providers manage schedules and rosters. Patient-facing widgets enable appointment booking externally, with all operations managed internally.

Results

Uberheal is actively used by healthcare teams in the US to manage daily operations. Internal usage data and feedback indicate faster appointment scheduling, improved outreach efficiency, and better visibility into referrals and campaigns. The platform has successfully replaced multiple legacy tools, reducing operational friction across clinics.

Reflection

Uberheal demonstrates how thoughtful UX can simplify complex healthcare operations. Designing for internal users—front desks, coordinators, and managers—highlighted the importance of clarity, speed, and role separation. This project strengthened my experience designing scalable, production-ready healthcare platforms.
