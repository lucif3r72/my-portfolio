Welspun Michigan — Immersive Infrastructure Experience

Welspun Michigan is a vertical of Welspun World, a global conglomerate operating across infrastructure and manufacturing. The objective was to design a modern digital experience that communicates complex infrastructure operations in a simple, engaging way. The centerpiece of the platform is an interactive 3D world that visually demonstrates how Welspun Michigan’s solutions operate across a city ecosystem.

  • CLIENT Welspun World (Welspun Michigan)
  • ROLE UI/UX Designer
  • PLATFORM Website
  • LIVE PROJECT In Production

Challenges

Infrastructure and industrial operations are complex and difficult to communicate through traditional websites. Stakeholders often struggle to understand how systems function at a city scale. The challenge was to translate technical processes into an experience that is intuitive, visually compelling, and accessible to decision-makers, partners, and the general audience.

Goals

The primary goal was to create a digital platform that clearly explains Welspun Michigan’s operations while reinforcing Welspun World’s scale and innovation leadership. A key objective was to use immersive 3D visuals to demonstrate infrastructure flow across an entire city—making complex concepts easy to grasp without technical jargon.

Design Highlights

The platform features a custom-built 3D city environment that animates how Welspun Michigan’s infrastructure solutions operate in real-world scenarios. Users can explore different parts of the city and understand system flow through guided animations and storytelling. The UI is clean and minimal to keep focus on the 3D experience while maintaining brand consistency and performance.

Results

The 3D-driven experience significantly improved engagement and understanding of Welspun Michigan’s operations. Stakeholders were able to visualize infrastructure impact at a city level, leading to clearer communication during presentations and discussions. The platform positioned Welspun Michigan as a forward-thinking and innovation-led enterprise.

Reflection

This project highlighted how immersive design can transform enterprise storytelling. By combining UX principles with 3D visualization, the platform bridges the gap between technical complexity and human understanding. It strengthened my experience designing high-impact digital experiences for large-scale organizations.
