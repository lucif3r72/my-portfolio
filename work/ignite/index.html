Ignite — Enterprise Idea & Innovation Management Platform

Ignite is a live, internal web platform used by Aditya Birla Hindalco to manage innovation initiatives at scale. The platform enables employees to submit ideas, respond to challenges, and collaborate within a structured governance framework. Designed with multi-role access, Ignite supports participants, reviewers, jurors, and administrators—ensuring transparency, accountability, and efficiency throughout the idea lifecycle.

  • CLIENT Aditya Birla Group (Hindalco)
  • ROLE UI/UX Designer
  • PLATFORM Web Application
  • LIVE PROJECT In Production (Enterprise)

Challenges

Large organizations generate many ideas, but managing them effectively is challenging. Traditional processes rely on emails, spreadsheets, and manual reviews—leading to low participation, poor visibility, and slow decision-making. The challenge was to design a platform that encourages idea submission while supporting structured review, scoring, and governance across multiple roles.

Goals

The goal was to create a centralized system for idea and challenge management that is easy for employees to use and powerful enough for enterprise governance. Participants needed a simple way to submit ideas or respond to challenges, while reviewers, jurors, and admins required clear workflows, scoring mechanisms, and role-based access control.

Design Highlights

Ignite is designed around role-specific dashboards that guide users through their responsibilities. Participants can create ideas or challenges with structured inputs. Reviewers and jurors access evaluation tools with scoring and feedback workflows. Admins oversee campaigns, roles, and progress analytics. The UI balances simplicity for contributors with control and visibility for decision-makers.

Results

Ignite is actively used within Hindalco to run internal innovation programs and idea campaigns. The platform improved participation rates, reduced review cycle time, and provided leadership with better visibility into innovation pipelines. It successfully replaced manual processes with a scalable digital system.

Reflection

Ignite highlights the importance of designing systems that balance creativity with structure. This project strengthened my experience in role-based UX, governance-driven workflows, and enterprise product design—where clarity, control, and adoption are equally critical.
