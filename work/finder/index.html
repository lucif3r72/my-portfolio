Finder — Smart Restaurant Discovery & Offers Platform

Finder is a conceptual restaurant discovery platform designed for the UAE market. It helps users find restaurants, lounges, cafés, and fine-dining experiences along with real-time offers and rewards. The product combines discovery, savings, and loyalty into a single seamless experience—allowing users to explore, apply discounts via QR codes, and earn reward coins for future redemptions.

  • CLIENT Concept Project
  • ROLE UI/UX Designer
  • PLATFORM Mobile App (iOS & Android)
  • PROJECT STATUS Conceptual (End-to-End Designed)

Challenges

The food discovery space is crowded with platforms that either focus on listings or discounts—but rarely both in a meaningful way. Users often struggle to compare prices, understand offers, and trust whether discounts will actually be honored at checkout. Designing a system that balances discovery, transparency, rewards, and in-person redemption without friction was the core challenge.

Goals

The goal was to design a platform that makes finding restaurants and saving money feel effortless. Finder needed to clearly surface offers, pricing for two people, ratings, and cuisine types while introducing a reliable QR-based discount redemption system. A long-term objective was to build a loyalty loop through coins that users can redeem for rewards, coupons, or brand offers.

Design Highlights

Finder uses a clean, card-based UI to highlight restaurants, categories, and trending offers. Restaurant detail pages focus on decision-making essentials—price range, ratings, offers, and cuisines. Discounts are applied through a QR code generated within the app, ensuring trust for both users and restaurants. A built-in rewards system converts savings and spend into coins, encouraging repeat usage.

Results

User testing and validation sessions showed high confidence in the QR-based redemption flow and strong engagement with the rewards system. Test users reported clearer decision-making and increased willingness to try new restaurants. Finder establishes a scalable foundation for partnerships, brand collaborations, and future geographic expansion.

Reflection

Finder explores how discovery, savings, and loyalty can coexist in a single experience. This project allowed me to design not just screens, but a complete value loop between users and restaurants. It reinforced the importance of trust, transparency, and simplicity—especially in everyday consumer products.
